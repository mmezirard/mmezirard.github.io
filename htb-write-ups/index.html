HTB Write-ups.

Hack The Box, abbreviated as HTB, brands itself as:

The #1 cybersecurity upskilling platform.

This is an online platform that provides a wide range of cybersecurity challenges and simulated environments for individuals to practice and improve their hacking skills, penetration testing abilities, and overall cybersecurity knowledge. HTB is designed to be a hands-on and immersive platform where users can learn by doing.
